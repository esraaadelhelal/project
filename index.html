OUR DOCTORS

what we offer

Oral and maxillary surgeries

These are surgeries concerned with treating upper jaw injuries, temporomandibular joint problems, jaw orthodontics, restoration surgeries, and wisdom tooth surgeries

Dental implant surgeries

These are surgeries aimed at replacing missing teeth, sinus lift surgeries, and bone grafting

Dental installation and replacement

This includes the installation of fixed and removable teeth, full and partial dentures, and the installation of dental crowns and bridges using materials made of porcelain and alloys made of metals that do not oxidize.

orthodontics

It is one of the methods that is used to treat various dental deformities, dental crowding, severe occlusion, open occlusion, and other deformities. The department has a special team of orthodontists permanently

Treating gum problems

The department performs many surgical and non-surgical procedures for treating gums, placing dental crowns, orthopedic surgeries, and cosmetic periodontal surgeries.

Dental problems in children

This includes surgical and non-surgical procedures to treat all dental problems and protect them from problems.
